Why we?

Meghe Group of Schools (MGS) is nurturing the future of more than 21,000 students across the state through the safe and caring environment conducive to learning and teaching. With years of experience within the educational space the MGS is now a network of 23 educational institutions, 21000 plus students and 1000 dedicated and loyal staff members. Our persistently growing network of education institutions includes a wide spectrum of Pre-Primary schools known as Meghe Pre Schools, CBSE schools known as School of Scholars and State Board Schools. Our academic record, innovative learning methods and strong commitment to delivering quality through integrity have made us leaders in the field of education within a short span. MGS today is a trusted name and an acknowledged leader in the field of education.

Our Inspiration

” Success is a journey to be achieved collectively…” Founder Chairman of Nagar Yuwak Shikshan Sanstha and Founder Chancellor of Datta Meghe Institute of Medical Sciences (Deemed University), Shri Dattaji Meghe is an exemplary of perseverance and determination. He has been active in the field of education for more than 30 years and has represented the people of Maharashtra in Lok Sabha for 3 consecutive terms.
